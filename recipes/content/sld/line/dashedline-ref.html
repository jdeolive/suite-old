Dashed Line

This example alters the Simple line to create a dashed line consisting of 5 pixels of drawn line alternating with 2 pixels of blank space.
In this example, line 10 sets the color of the lines to be blue (#0000FF) and line 11 sets the width of the lines to be 3 pixels. Line 12 determines the composition of the line dashes. The value of 5 2 creates a repeating pattern of 5 pixels of drawn line, followed by 2 pixels of omitted line.
